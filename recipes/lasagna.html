Lasagna Recipe

Indulge in the comforting layers of our Classic Homemade Lasagna, a timeless recipe that brings together the richness of perfectly seasoned ground beef, savory tomato sauce, and velvety layers of lasagna noodles. Each bite is a symphony of flavors, with the creamy goodness of ricotta and mozzarella cheeses melding seamlessly with the robust taste of marinara. Baked to golden perfection, this lasagna is a labor of love that captures the essence of home-cooked Italian cuisine. Whether you're hosting a family dinner or craving a hearty meal, our Classic Homemade Lasagna is sure to be a crowd-pleaser, leaving everyone longing for seconds. Elevate your culinary skills with this beloved recipe that stands the test of time.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • onion
  • garlic
  • red wine or broth
  • marinara sauce
  • lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • ricotta cheese
  • egg

Steps

  1. Brown the beef – Add oil to a deep pan and sautee onion and beef until browned, 5 minutes then add garlic and stir another minute.
  2. Finish the sauce – Pour in 1/4 cup of wine and stir until almost evaporated. Add marinara, salt, pepper, thyme, sugar, and parsley; bring to a simmer, then cover and cook 5 minutes.Finish the sauce – Pour in 1/4 cup of wine and stir until almost evaporated. Add marinara, salt, pepper, thyme, sugar, and parsley; bring to a simmer, then cover and cook 5 minutes.
  3. Make the cheese filling – Combine all of the cheese sauce ingredients and 1 cup of mozzarella in a mixing bowl (reserve the rest for later).
  4. Prep – Preheat your oven to 375ºF and cook the lasagna noodles in a pot of well-salted water until al dente.
  5. Assemble – Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9×13-inch casserole dish. Add 3 noodles, followed by 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1 cup of mozzarella, and half of the ricotta mixture. Repeat, then for the top layer, add 3 noodles, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella.
  6. Bake – Cover with foil and bake at 375˚F for 45 minutes, then remove the foil and broil for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the cheese is lightly browned.
  7. Rest – Let the lasagna rest for about 30 minutes before cutting and serving.
